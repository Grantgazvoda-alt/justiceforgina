REPORTING FRAMEWORK

Language that preserves the record.

DOCUMENTED

Use direct attribution

  • “The signed probate decree states…”
  • “The police narrative records…”
  • “The retained examiner opined…”
FAMILY POSITION

Identify the speaker

  • “Gina's sons believe…”
  • “The family alleges…”
  • “The family requests investigation of…”
NOT ESTABLISHED

Do not collapse distinctions

  • Do not describe allegations as verdicts.
  • Do not treat estate fraud as proof of homicide.
  • Do not infer current investigative status without official confirmation.
