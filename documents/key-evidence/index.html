PROVENANCE METHOD

How packet contents become trustworthy public records.

STEP ONE

Identify the generating source

Determine whether each page was generated by a court, police agency, EMS provider, funeral business, medical office, family member, attorney, or another source.

STEP TWO

Separate record from interpretation

Record what the page actually states, then place family conclusions, expert analysis, legal argument, and unresolved questions in distinct layers.

STEP THREE

Verify and redact

Confirm dates, case numbers, authorship, signatures, page sequence, and context; remove unrelated identifiers and sensitive details before creating a local public derivative.

STEP FOUR

State the limitation

Every public page explains what its source establishes and what remains unknown. A record may document a statement without proving that the statement is accurate.
