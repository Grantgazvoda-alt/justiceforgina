OFFICIAL RECORDS · PRIVACY-PROTECTED EXTRACT

Police and EMS recorded different accounts of the same absence.

The Waterbury Police incident report and Trinity Health of New England EMS patient-care report were created from the November 21, 2021 response. The records describe different explanations for where Nicholas Forte had been before returning and finding Gina unresponsive.

What this establishes:The two official narratives are not identical. It does not establish why they differ, whether either was misunderstood, or whether the discrepancy reflects criminal conduct.

Waterbury Police narrative

The police report states that Forte said he left the house at approximately 6:00 a.m. for work and returned at approximately 7:00 p.m., when he found Gina unconscious and not breathing on the couch.

UNRESOLVED

What requires investigation

The records do not explain whether the different statements resulted from separate conversations, misunderstanding, incomplete note-taking, a later clarification, or a materially inconsistent account.

  • Interview the report authors and relevant witnesses.
  • Compare original audio, dispatch, phone, work, travel, and location records.
  • Determine whether either account was corrected or supplemented.
  • Avoid treating the discrepancy alone as proof of guilt.
