SWORN STATEMENTS

What an affidavit establishes

It records statements sworn or affirmed by the declarant under the terms shown in the document.

NOT A VERDICT

What it does not establish

Filing or signing an affidavit does not make every statement independently verified or create a final judicial finding.

ATTRIBUTION

How it must be quoted

Statements must be attributed to the named declarant and checked against records, testimony, corrections, and court rulings.
