SOURCE-QUALITY MATRIX

Not every sentence in a draft has the same evidentiary weight.

FIRSTHAND ACCOUNT

Potential direct testimony

A proposed declarant's account of a call, conversation, observation, or event in which that person directly participated. It remains the declarant's testimony unless independently corroborated.

ATTRIBUTED STATEMENT

Secondhand or hearsay content

A statement describing what another person allegedly said. It must name the source, preserve attribution, and be checked against that person's testimony or contemporaneous record.

RECORD-DEPENDENT CLAIM

Requires underlying documentation

A statement about banking, insurance, medical treatment, legal authority, account values, forms, or official actions that should not be published as verified without the source record.

EDITORIAL OR UNRESOLVED

Not publication-ready

Text accompanied by requests to confirm, omit, correct, add a date, add a name, or reconcile newer evidence. These items remain excluded from verified public claims.
