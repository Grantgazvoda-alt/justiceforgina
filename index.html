THE STANDARD

Document first.
Conclude carefully.

This project separates what a record verifies from what remains alleged, disputed, restricted, or understood by the family.

01

Source every factual claim

Whenever lawful and responsible, readers are directed to the underlying record or source packet.

02

Label unresolved matters

Arrests, charges, family statements, and disputed events are not presented as final court findings.

03

Protect private people

Personal addresses, private contact information, medical identifiers, and unrelated sensitive details are not published.

04

Preserve context

Documents are organized chronologically and accompanied by status, category, and publication notes.

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Clarity matters.

Does this website claim anyone murdered Gina?

No. The family has serious concerns and seeks further review, but this site does not present an unproven homicide allegation as an established fact. Criminal responsibility can be determined only through lawful investigation and court proceedings.

Does the arrest prove the forgery charges?

No. Arrest and charging records establish that accusations are pending; they do not establish guilt. The accused is presumed innocent unless proven guilty.

Why are some records restricted?

Medical, personal, privileged, sealed, or third-party-sensitive records may contain information that should not be posted publicly. Restricted material may be preserved for authorized legal or expert review.

How can the public help responsibly?

Share verified updates, refer qualified journalists or attorneys, connect the family with reputable advocacy organizations, and support the fundraiser after reviewing it independently. Do not threaten, harass, contact, or publish private information about any person involved.
