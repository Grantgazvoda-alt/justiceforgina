FREQUENTLY ASKED

Direct questions deserve direct answers.

Is the family saying Nicholas Forte killed Gina?

The family states that it believes he may have been responsible and wants that possibility investigated as a potential homicide. That is an attributed allegation and family conclusion—not an established legal fact.

Does this site prove homicide?

No. The materials raise questions and support the family's request for investigation. Criminal causation and guilt can be determined only through lawful investigation and court proceedings.

Why publish the documents?

Direct access allows journalists, attorneys, advocates, experts, and the public to evaluate the family's claims against the underlying material rather than relying on summaries alone.

How can people help responsibly?

Share the official site, refer qualified professionals, support lawful records work, and avoid threatening, harassing, doxxing, or contacting people named in the materials.
